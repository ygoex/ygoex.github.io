Spanish Language Tutor


A Spanish language tutor required a website to promote and set her services apart from the competition.

One of the reasons why British citizens decide to learn Spanish is to enhance their experience during holidays.

As the favourite travel destinations in Spain are those by the beach, this topic served as inspiration for choosing the photographs of the website.

The orange color was elected for its association with the Spanish warm and sunny weather.

Astrophysics Researcher


A researcher in astrophysics presented the challenge to design and develop a portfolio with the following specifications:

  • Static website.
  • Minimalistic design and clean appearance.
  • Don't rely on javascript for core site functionality.

The visual experience was enriched by using one of the researcher awarded photographs. Having taken the color of the sky in the photograph as the main color of the website, two analogous colors (midnight blue and teal) were added to create an harmonious palette.
